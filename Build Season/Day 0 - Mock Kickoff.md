## Full Day Mock Kickoff Schedule

If kickoff occurs **during** winter break

|   Time   | Duration |                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| :------: | :------: | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 9:00 am  |  15 min  | Students arrive <br>discuss how holiday break was                                                                                                                                 |
| 9:15 am  |  10 min  | Show Videos:<br>[SuperNURDs 10 Year Anniversary](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W35IwdE_Zh0)<br>[Robowranglers: Fail Again](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8yn2CbQUlTI)         |
| 9:25 am  |  1.5 hr  | Discuss build season expectations and schedule                                                                                                                                    |
| 10:45 am |  30 min  | Watch Game Animation & Field Videos<br>Take notes                                                                                                                                 |
| 11:15 am |  10 min  | Unbox KoP (Put old game pieces in tote and pass around)                                                                                                                           |
| 11:25 am |  45 min  | [Game Manual Breakdown - Reading](Day%201.1%20-%20Game%20Manual%20Breakdown.md#Reading)                                                                   |
| 12:10 pm |  50 min  | Lunch Break (spill over)                                                                                                                                                          |
| 1:00 pm  |  50 min  | [Game Manual Breakdown - Presenting](Day%201.1%20-%20Game%20Manual%20Breakdown.md#Presenting)                                                                    |
| 2:00 pm  |   1 hr   | [Scoring method Analysis](Day%201.2%20-%20Scoring%20Method%20Analysis.md) <br>(**ranking points vs match points**)<br>(point calculations/combinations, look at density, looking at auto vs teleop)<br>(Do RP become match points?) |
| 3:00 pm  |  1.5 hr  | [Feature importance List creation](Day%201.3%20-%20Feature%20Importance%20List.md) <br>(features are ability that our robot has that drive the design) <br>(ex. score high, mid, low, all autonomous)                               |
| 4:30 pm  |  15 min  | Q&A and debrief                                                                                                                                                                   |
| 4:45 pm  |  45 min  | Feature importance list rankings (Monday Practice)                                                                                                                                |
| 5:30 pm  |  15 min  | Game piece interaction prototyping and Video/part research                                                                                                                        |
| 5:45 pm  |   1 hr   | General Robot Architecture (Monday Practice)                                                                                                                                      |
| 6:45 pm  |    -     | End Mock Kick off                                                                                                                                                                 |
