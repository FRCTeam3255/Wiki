## Competition Day Checklist

### 1. Load in

1. Have proper plan for getting robot ready, tuned radio etc.
2. Have mallets out and in car cabin
3. Truck driver stays at trailer coordinates unloading
4. Unloading means quickly bringing into pit and coming back to grab more
5. **Right off the trailer, immediately take the robot to radio configuration (see Section 2 below)**
6. Bring out structure first start setting up asap
7. Once structure is up with shelves, bring in electrical and mechanical toolboxes
8. Start bringing totes/other items in on dollies and carts
9. Unpack carts and bring back to trailer for another load
10. Make sure truck, trailer, and truck bed are fully unpacked
11. When unpacking totes, set in middle, one or two people take stuff out, saying what shelf they go on, and then another person standing stationary places in the shelf
12. Make stack of empty totes outside of pit
13. Stack items that need to be brought back to trailer on gray dolly
14. Bring gray dolly/other items back to trailer
15. Park trailer and truck

### 2. Radio Configuration
- Bring a second radio for easier swapping.
- **Remember to obtain the robot Wi-Fi password while configuring radio.**
- After configuring the radio, **take a photo and send it in Slack**.

### 3. Inspection

After completing radio configuration, take the robot directly to inspection.

1. Weigh the robot on the official inspection scale.
2. **Take a photo of the weight reading and send it in Slack.**
3. Complete the full inspection checklist with the inspector.

### 4. Field Calibration
**Supplies Needed:**

- Ethernet spool
- Hot glue gun
- Tape measure
- One of each scoring element
- Programming laptop
- Battery-powered Ethernet switch

**Steps:**

1. Follow the Calibration Guide for the Robot (This is in the robot's `README.md`)
2. Limelights can use USB: [http://172.28.0.1:5801](http://172.28.0.1:5801)

### 5. End of Competition
- Reprogram the radio back to normal settings.
- [See Radio Reset Instructions](../Software/Configuring%20the%20Radio.md)
- Make robot fixes (as if we are going to another match)

### 6. Load out

We pack the pit **after finals** are complete.

#### Pit Packing

1. Set some mallets and flush cutters aside
1. Set aside step ladder
1. Set aside broom and dustpan
1. Layout all totes in numbered order
2. Put tubs in totes according to their number
   - Close totes once complete
3. Set aside boxes without a tote label until later
4. Get gray dolly and stack all completed totes (2x3 totes)
4. Put quick grab hardware (i.e. rivets) in tool boxes
4. Move tool boxes out of pits
5. Clip shelving zip ties
6. Make stack of wood shelves
7. Remove laptop and controllers from driver stations
8. Pack controllers in special box
9. Take sign off pit via step ladder (4 person job)
10. Take down rest of structure
11. Stats box goes in trailer trunk
12. Media stuff stays with media car (tripod goes in trailer trunk)
13. Sweep up pits
