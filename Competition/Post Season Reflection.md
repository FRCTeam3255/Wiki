# Post Season Reflection

Do this in Slack.

## Team Goals

1. Go over lifetime goals.
2. Go over next-season overarching goals.
3. Go over driving goals (sub-goals that support overarching goals).
4. Go into detail on broad ideas or issues that affect those driving goals.
5. Go over only the 3 largest pain points to stay focused.

## Student Goals

1. Go around and have students share career goals (this helps determine where to place students on the robot/project).
2. Mentors give life lessons, advice, and anecdotes related to student career goals.
3. Mentors talk through career paths and options (there is not one required path; students do not all need a specific college or internship path).
