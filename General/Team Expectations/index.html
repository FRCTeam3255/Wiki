Team Expectations

  • Please come to all meetings that you can, we are a team working together for a common goal
    • If you cannot attend a meeting (or will be late) please fill out the absent form
    • The more you put in the more you get out
    • If you have to miss a meeting make sure you have documented what works needs to get done when you arent present
  • Make sure all work assigned to you is complete
    • We unfortunately can't afford to bring everyone to comp, so role responsbility/attendance plays a big part
    • Don't volunteer to own something if you aren't sure you can complete it
    • Anyone can step up and become a leader!
    • If you have to leave before the task is completed or have to miss a meeting please find someone to cover you/take over and notify a mentor
  • Use meetings as time to ask mentors questions and get feedback
    • Try to complete individual items between meetings
  • Write summaries of what you did at each meeting in Slack before leaving
    1. What you completed? (Posted in Slack, make sure you uploaded to GitHub)
    2. What needs to get done next
  • Avoid blockers! - Make sure stuff is ordered as soon as possible
  • Think "scrappy" and creative to get stuff done, don't wait to do stuff
  • When you leave, you'll be asked
    1. What you completed? (Posted in Slack, make sure you uploaded to GitHub)
    2. Who picked up after you/what's your plan to get it done
    3. Did you clean your area and put your toolkit and project boxes away
  • Keep the shop neat and orderly
    • Designated cleaning times: (Everyone)
      • Tuesdays/Wednesdays 7:35pm to 7:45pm
      • Fridays/Saturdays 8:45pm to 9pm
    • Use 1 colored coded workspace per project, only using the corresponding toolbox
    • Make sure all of your item go into your project box
  • Shutdown and upload files from the laptop you were using
    • Help to empty trash/recycle when you see it full
    • Park robots correctly when the day ends/leaving
    • Throw away water bottles
    • Push in chairs and keep them out of walkway
    • Clean up all wiring messes
  • Do not play video games on any devices
  • No yelling unless there is danger or someone is hurt
  • Back packs in the coding cave
  • Maintain good grades, school comes first!
  • Have fun, but be professional
  • Follow shop safety rules
